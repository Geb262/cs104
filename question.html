Questions and Answers

Our team is comprised of four members who understand the struggle of not getting enough sleep. Because of this, we wanted to construct a website that would help us know how much sleep we get, along with some helpful tips and seeing how our sleep may improve over time.

Anyone and everyone can benefit from our site. This program was built to help improve the sleep schedule of many, so whether you are a new parent, a college student, or a first responder, we hope our website can help you.

We record the data from the page you set up right before going to sleep. On that page, we get the data from how long you are recording, from there, we translate that data to rem cycles to understand how much sleep you got. That is the only data we track. We do not sell your data.

We try to provide minor updates around twice a month, cleaning up bugs, and combining data. We tend to do big updates about once every two months, providing new features to improve our site.
